MY FAVORATE MOVIES LIST

  1. MS Dhoni: The Untold Story

    • Released IN :- 2016
    • Sushant Singh Rajput, Kiara Advani, Disha Patani
    • This biographical sports film chronicles the life of Mahendra Singh Dhoni, the former captain of the Indian national cricket team, highlighting his journey from a small-town boy to a cricket legend.


  2. Chhichhore

    • Released IN :- 2019
    • Sushant Singh Rajput, Shraddha Kapoor
    • A heartwarming dramedy that revolves around a group of friends from college who reunite years later.


  3. Kedarnath

    • Released IN :- 2018
    • Sushant Singh Rajput, Sara Ali Khan
    • A romantic drama set against the backdrop of the 2013 Uttarakhand floods. The film tells the story of a Muslim porter and a Hindu tourist who fall in love amidst the natural calamity, showcasing themes of love, faith, and resilience.


  4. Bajrangi Bhaijaan

    • Released IN :- 2015
    • Salman Khan, Harshaali Malhotra, Kareena Kapoor Khan
    • A touching drama about a devout Hanuman devotee, Pavan (Salman Khan), who embarks on a journey to reunite a mute Pakistani girl with her family across the border.


  5. Tanhaji: The Unsung Warrior

    • Released IN :- 2020
    • Ajay Devgn, Saif Ali Khan, Kajol
    • A historical drama based on the life of Tanaji Malusare, a Maratha warrior and military leader. Ajay Devgn's portrayal of the titular character, along with impressive action scenes and visual effects, made this film a major hit.
